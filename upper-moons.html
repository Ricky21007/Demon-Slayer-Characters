Upper Moons

Muzan Kibutsuji

Upper Moon

Background

The first demon, driven to achieve immortality and invincibility.

Family

Adopts human disguises, has a faux family (wife and daughter).

Rank

King of Demons, leader of all demonic forces.

Physical Abilities

Immense strength, regeneration, shape-shifting, and speed.

Blood Demon Art

Black Blood: Brambles, lethal whip-like attacks, biological manipulation.

Forms

Normal Form: Human-like appearance for disguise.
Combat Form: Enhanced limbs, whip-like appendages.
Final Form: Grotesque, massive body with mouths and tentacles.
