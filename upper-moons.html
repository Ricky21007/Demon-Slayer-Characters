Upper Moons

Muzan Kibutsuji

Demon King

The first demon, driven to achieve immortality and invincibility.

Family: Adopts human disguises, has a faux family (wife and daughter).

Physical Abilities: Immense strength, regeneration, shape-shifting, and speed.

Blood Demon Art: Black Blood: Brambles

Forms: Normal Form: Human-like appearance; Combat Form: Enhanced limbs, whip-like appendages; Final Form: Grotesque, massive body with mouths and tentacles.

Kokushibo

Upper Rank One

Former Demon Slayer Michikatsu Tsugikuni, turned demon out of envy.

Family: Brother: Yoriichi Tsugikuni; family from human life deceased.

Physical Abilities: Immense strength, speed, and swordsmanship.

Blood Demon Art: Crescent Moon Blades

Forms: Normal Form: Six-eyed, samurai-like appearance; Enhanced Form: Monstrous, blade-covered body.

Doma

Upper Rank Two

A nihilistic demon, born with rainbow eyes, led a cult as a human.

Family: Parents saw him as divine, started a cult around him.

Physical Abilities: High strength, speed, and regeneration.

Blood Demon Art: Frozen Lotus

Forms: Single demon form, no major transformations.

Akaza

Upper Rank Three

A martial artist turned demon, seeks strong opponents.

Family: Father, fiancée Koyuki, and her father killed in human life.

Physical Abilities: Exceptional strength, speed, and martial arts skill.

Blood Demon Art: Destructive Death

Forms: Single demon form, no major transformations.

Hantengu

Upper Rank Four

A cowardly demon who splits into emotion-based clones.

Family: Human family unknown.

Physical Abilities: Regeneration, splitting into multiple bodies.

Blood Demon Art: Emotion Manifestation

Forms: Normal Form: Small, frail appearance; Clones: Sekido (anger), Karaku (pleasure), Aizetsu (sorrow), Urogi (joy), Urami (resentment); Zohakuten: Merged, powerful dragon form.

Gyokko

Upper Rank Five

A grotesque, art-obsessed demon with a love for pots.

Family: Human family unknown.

Physical Abilities: Regeneration, flexibility, and aquatic movement.

Blood Demon Art: Porcelain Vases

Forms: Normal Form: Pot-bound, scaly appearance; True Form: Serpentine, scaled body.

Daki

Upper Rank Six

A cruel demon, works with brother Gyutaro in the Entertainment District.

Family: Brother: Gyutaro; human family deceased.

Physical Abilities: Enhanced strength, speed, and regeneration.

Blood Demon Art: Obi Sash

Forms: Normal Form: Beautiful, courtesan-like appearance.

Gyutaro

Upper Rank Six

Daki's protective brother, a vicious demon from the Entertainment District.

Family: Sister: Daki; human family deceased.

Physical Abilities: Immense strength, speed, and poison-based attacks.

Blood Demon Art: Blood Sickle

Forms: Normal Form: Gaunt, scarred appearance emerging from Daki.

Kaigaku

Upper Rank Six

A traitorous Demon Slayer turned demon, betrayed Jigoro.

Family: Human family unknown; orphaned.

Physical Abilities: Enhanced speed, strength, and swordsmanship.

Blood Demon Art: Electrokinetic Thunder Breathing

Forms: Normal Form: Demonized appearance with black markings.
